## Request

Pursuant to the Illinois Freedom of Information Act (5 ILCS 140), I respectfully
request the following records related to the Cook County Sheriff's Office
deployment on Chicago Transit Authority property, including the security
arrangement announced in March 2026.

### Record Set 1: Contract and Cost

1. Any contract, memorandum of understanding, intergovernmental agreement, or
   other governing document between the Cook County Sheriff's Office and the
   Chicago Transit Authority related to transit security services, for the
   period 2023 through present
2. Total dollar value of each such agreement
3. Reimbursement rate (per hour, per officer, or other unit) paid by CTA to
   the Sheriff's Office under each agreement
4. Total hours committed or invoiced under each agreement by calendar year

### Record Set 2: Activity Breakdown

For each calendar year covered by the agreements above, records sufficient
to show officer hours broken out by activity type, specifically:

1. Hours spent on fare enforcement activity (farecard inspection, fare evasion
   enforcement, or equivalent)
2. Hours spent on general patrol on CTA rail lines and stations
3. Hours spent on crime response (assault, robbery, battery, or equivalent)
4. Any other activity categories tracked under the CTA deployment

If the Sheriff's Office does not track activity hours by the specific categories
listed above, please provide whatever deployment logs or activity records exist
for the CTA assignment, along with documentation of the tracking system used.

### Record Set 3: Outcomes

For each calendar year of CTA deployment:

1. Number of arrests made on CTA property by Sheriff's deputies, broken out
   by charge category
2. Number of fare evasion citations issued by Sheriff's deputies
3. Number of individuals identified with outstanding warrants during CTA
   deployment
