## Request

Pursuant to the Illinois Freedom of Information Act (5 ILCS 140), I respectfully
request the following records related to transit-related Administrative Notice of
Violation (ANOV) citations adjudicated by the Department of Administrative Hearings.

### Record Set 1: Citation Volume and Disposition

For each calendar year 2018 through present, records sufficient to show:

1. Total number of transit-related ANOV citations received by DOAH, broken out
   by violation code and violation description
2. Disposition of each citation category: liable, not liable, dismissed,
   defaulted, continued, or otherwise resolved
3. Dollar amount of fines assessed in each disposition category

Transit-related violation codes of interest include but are not limited to
Chicago Municipal Code sections 9-80, 36-008, and 36-009. If DOAH uses
different internal code classifications for CTA or transit-related violations,
please apply those classifications.

### Record Set 2: Payment and Collection

For each calendar year 2018 through present, records sufficient to show:

1. Dollar amount of transit-related fines actually collected (cash received)
2. Dollar amount of transit-related fines outstanding or sent to collections
3. Dollar amount of transit-related fines waived, reduced, or written off
4. Collection rate (collected / assessed) if tracked as an internal metric

### Record Set 3: Administrative Cost

Records sufficient to show the per-case administrative cost attributable to
processing a transit-related ANOV citation through DOAH, including:

1. Any internal cost-per-hearing estimates or efficiency studies
2. Staff time allocations for transit citation hearings if tracked separately
   from other citation categories
